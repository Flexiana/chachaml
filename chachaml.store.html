chachaml.store

Store constructor dispatcher.

(open opts) creates the right backend based on :type:

(open {:type :sqlite})                    ; default
(open {:type :sqlite :path "my.db"})
(open {:type :postgres :jdbc-url "jdbc:postgresql://..."})

The rest of chachaml doesn’t care which backend is active — it talks through the protocols in chachaml.store.protocol.

open

(open)(open {store-type :type, :or {store-type :sqlite}, :as opts})

Open a store. Dispatches on :type (default :sqlite).

SQLite options: :path, :in-memory?, :artifact-dir Postgres options: :jdbc-url, :username, :password, :artifact-dir, :pool-size
